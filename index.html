To Newcomers

We understand that Brockian Ultra-Cricket can seem complicated at first. However, the rules to the game are actually very simple!

Rules

1. The first, and most important rules of Brockian Ultra-Cricket is that each and every player must grow at least three additional legs.

2. If possible, find one good Brockian Ultra-Cricket player and clone them several few times. This should save an enormous amount of tedious selection and training.

3. Place both teams in a large field, with a high wall around them. This way, spectators of the sport will believe the gameplay to be far more exciting then it really is.

4. Throw assorted items of sporting equipment over the walls surrounding the field. Anything will do - cricket bats, basecube bats, tennis guns, skis etc.

5. Players should start acquiring 'hits' on the opposing team. When a hit is scored, players should run away and apologise from a safe distance. Apologies should be concise, sincere, and for maximum points, through a megaphone.

6. The winning team shall be the first team that wins.
